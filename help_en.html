■About Offline Mode


[Character illustrations]
・All available illustrations are of the characters after max Limit Break.
・Characters are at max level.
・All Skill Slots and unlockable attributes are unlocked.
・All upgradable characters are fully upgraded. This cannot be reversed.
・You cannot transfer any characters you obtained as of end-of-service to the offline version.

[Quests]
・Items are not acquired when you complete quests.
・As a matter of convenience, all items are shown as Cols.
・As a matter of convenience, the Memory Diamond display shows 1,000 in your possession.
・You cannot actually use Memory Diamonds.
・Memory Diamonds are not required to continue quests.
・You cannot do quests in Multi-Play.
・Ultimate Fight Access Keys are not required to play Ultimate Fight battles.

[Playback of stories]
・Limited event stories are not available.
・Main stories that you did not complete before end-of-service are also available.
